Basic Info About Me

I'm an atmospheric scientist who loves to have fun indoor and outdoor!

I specialise in cloud microphysics, atmospheric thermodynamics and climate processes.

My research interests include climate change impacts on water cycle and forest-atmosphere relationships.

I love travelling and I am keen on exploring and learning new languages and cultures.
